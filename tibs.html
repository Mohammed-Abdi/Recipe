Tibs: Ethiopian Sautéed Meat Dish
Tibs is a popular Ethiopian dish made of sautéed beef, lamb, or chicken, cooked with spices and vegetables. It's often served with injera and enjoyed during special occasions or as a hearty meal.
Ingredient Quantity
Beef, lamb, or chicken (cut into cubes) 1 lb
Onion (chopped) 1 large
Garlic (minced) 3 cloves
Tomatoes (chopped) 2 medium
Green chili peppers (optional) 2
Turmeric powder 1 teaspoon
Berbere (spice blend) 1 tablespoon
Butter or vegetable oil 2 tablespoons
Salt To taste

Instructions

1. Sauté the Onions and Garlic

Heat butter or oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, sautéing until softened and golden, about 5–7 minutes.

2. Add Tomatoes and Spices

Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, and berbere. Stir well and cook for another 5 minutes, allowing the tomatoes to break down.

3. Cook the Meat

Add the cubed meat (beef, lamb, or chicken) to the pan. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the meat is browned and cooked through, about 10–15 minutes.

4. Add Green Chili (Optional)

If you like extra spice, add the sliced green chili peppers and cook for another 2-3 minutes.

5. Season and Serve

Season with salt to taste and serve your Tibs hot with injera or rice.
