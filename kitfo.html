Kitfo: Ethiopian Minced Meat Dish
Kitfo is a traditional Ethiopian dish made from finely minced raw or cooked beef, seasoned with a blend of spices, and often served with injera. It’s a flavorful, rich dish often enjoyed during special occasions.
Ingredient Quantity
Beef (finely minced) 1 lb
Niter Kibbeh (spiced butter) 1/4 cup
Berbere (spice blend) 1 teaspoon
Cardamom powder 1/2 teaspoon
Salt To taste
Injera (for serving) Optional, as a side

Instructions

1. Prepare the Meat

Finely mince the beef. If you prefer cooked Kitfo, lightly sauté the meat until browned. For raw Kitfo, ensure the beef is very fresh and clean.

2. Prepare the Niter Kibbeh

In a small pan, melt the Niter Kibbeh over low heat and add the berbere and cardamom powder. Stir and cook for 2-3 minutes to infuse the spices into the butter.

3. Combine Meat and Spices

Mix the minced beef with the spiced Niter Kibbeh, adding salt to taste. If you're using cooked meat, ensure the mixture is well combined and warmed through.

4. Serve

Serve Kitfo with injera, and optionally top with a small amount of gomen (steamed greens) or ayib (Ethiopian cheese) for added flavor.
