About Las Recipe

Welcome to Las, your go-to destination for authentic Ethiopian recipes that bring the heart of Ethiopia to your kitchen. Our mission is to share the bold, aromatic, and flavorful dishes of Ethiopian cuisine, from traditional stews like Doro Wat to vibrant vegetarian dishes like Misir Wat and Shiro Wat. We believe in the power of food to connect people and celebrate culture.

At Las, we are passionate about providing not just recipes, but stories behind each dish. Whether you are a seasoned cook or a beginner, our easy-to-follow recipes will guide you through creating mouth-watering meals that will transport you straight to Ethiopia.

Our Story

Las was founded with the goal of showcasing the diverse and rich culinary traditions of Ethiopia. Named after the word “Las,” meaning “lick” in Amharic, we strive to make every recipe not just a meal, but an experience. Our team works to highlight the unique flavors, spices, and ingredients that make Ethiopian food special.

Why Choose Las?

  • Authentic, traditional Ethiopian recipes.
  • Step-by-step instructions for all skill levels.
  • Vibrant food photography to inspire your cooking.
  • Dedicated to sharing the culture, stories, and love behind each dish.
