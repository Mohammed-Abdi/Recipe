Tikil Gomen: Ethiopia's Cabbage Stew
Tikil Gomen is a mild and flavorful Ethiopian dish made with cabbage, carrots, and potatoes, seasoned with spices. It’s a comforting dish often enjoyed as a side or main dish.
Ingredient Quantity
Cabbage (chopped) 1 small head
Carrots (sliced) 2 large
Potatoes (diced) 2 medium
Onion (chopped) 1 medium
Garlic (minced) 3 cloves
Turmeric powder 1 teaspoon
Cumin powder 1 teaspoon
Coriander powder 1/2 teaspoon
Vegetable oil 2 tablespoons
Salt To taste
Water 2 cups

Instructions

1. Prepare the Vegetables

Chop the cabbage, slice the carrots, and dice the potatoes. Set aside.

2. Sauté the Onion and Garlic

In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil and sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until softened and fragrant, about 5–7 minutes.

3. Add the Spices

Stir in the turmeric, cumin, and coriander powders. Cook for 1 minute to release the flavors.

4. Add the Vegetables

Add the cabbage, carrots, and potatoes to the pot. Stir well to coat with the spices.

5. Add Water and Simmer

Pour in the water, bring to a simmer, and cover the pot. Let it cook for 20–25 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

6. Season and Serve

Adjust the salt to taste, then serve Tikil Gomen hot with injera or rice.
